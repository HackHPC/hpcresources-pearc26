Frequently Asked Questions

A quick guide to everything on this page — search, filters, sharing, mentors, and speakers.

Resource Directory

How do I search for a resource?
Type into the search box at the top of the Resource Directory tab. It matches against each resource's title, category, and description as you type — no need to press Enter.
How do I filter by category or by major?

Click "Filter by category…" or "Filter by major…" to open a checklist. Check as many boxes as you like — a resource shows up if it matches any checked category, or any checked major (checking boxes in both narrows results to resources matching both at once). Use "Clear selection" at the top of the panel to reset just that filter.

How do I sort the list?
Use the "Sort" dropdown on the right of the filter bar to switch between alphabetical order (Name A–Z) and grouping by category (Category A–Z, then Name A–Z within each category).
What do the badges on a resource card mean?

The small labels above a resource's title are its categories — a resource can have more than one.

  • 🎪 PEARC EXHIBITOR (violet/fuchsia badge with a matching left border) marks vendors and organizations exhibiting on-site at PEARC26.
  • ⚡ Mentorship Available (orange, pulsing) appears when a resource has a named contact person — click their name to jump to their profile on the Connected Mentors tab.
How do I share a single resource?

Click "Share" in the top-right of any resource card. On a phone or supported browser this opens your device's native share sheet; otherwise it opens a small menu — copy link, text message, email, X, LinkedIn, or Facebook. The copied/shared link jumps straight back to that resource, highlighted, when opened.

How do I select and share multiple resources at once?

Check the small checkbox in the top-right of any resource card — it works across searches and filters, so you can check resources, then search for more, without losing your selections. The "Share Multiple Resources" button above the search bar shows a live count; click it to review your list and choose how to export it:

  • Download PDF — opens a printable page; use your browser's print dialog to save it as a PDF.
  • Download CSV / Download Markdown — saves a file with each resource's name, URL, categories, and description.
  • Copy to Clipboard — copies the same info as plain text.
  • Share — opens your device's native share sheet, or copies to clipboard if that's not available.

Use "Clear All Selected" to uncheck everything and start over.

How do I suggest a new resource?
Click "➕ Suggest a Resource" in the header and fill out the short form. Submitting opens a pre-filled GitHub Issue on the project's repository for a HackHPC admin to review and add.

Connected Mentors

How do I browse or contact a mentor?

Switch to the Connected Mentors tab to see every verified mentor, their affiliation (linked to their organization's site), and their focus area. Use "Connect on LinkedIn" or "✉️ Email" on their card to reach out directly.

How do I become a Connected Mentor myself?
Fill out the "Become a Connected Mentor" form on the left side of the Connected Mentors tab. Submitting opens a pre-filled GitHub Issue for a HackHPC admin to verify and add you to the roster.

Groundwork for Greatness

Where do I find the speaker bios, and how do I contact a speaker?
The Groundwork for Greatness tab lists every speaker in the morning speaker series, with their session time, affiliations (linked), and a short bio. Use "Connect on LinkedIn" or "✉️ Email" on their card to reach out.
